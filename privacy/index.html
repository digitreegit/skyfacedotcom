Skyface, LLC (“Skyface”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the website skyface.com and develops software applications and digital products (collectively, the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy describes how Skyface handles information in connection with its Services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Some Skyface products may allow users to enter data directly into the product.
This information is processed solely for the purpose of providing the product’s functionality.

Skyface does notrequire users to create accounts unless explicitly stated within a specific product.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Skyface does notcollect or track:

  • Personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Cross-app or cross-website activity

Skyface does notuse tracking technologies, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics services by default.

2. How Information Is Used

Information processed within Skyface products is used exclusively to:

  • Provide core product functionality
  • Maintain user preferences
  • Improve usability and performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Skyface products are designed to minimize data collection.

  • Data may be stored locally on a user’s device, depending on the product
  • Skyface does not access user data unless explicitly stated by the product
  • Users remain in control of any data they choose to export or manage

4. Third-Party Services

Skyface Services do not integrate with third-party advertising networks or data brokers.

If a product links to an external service, that service is governed by its own privacy policy.

5. Children’s Privacy

Skyface Services are not intended for children under the age of 13.
Skyface does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Choices

Depending on the product, users may be able to:

  • Remove data by uninstalling the product
  • Manage preferences within the product
  • Export or delete stored data

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last updated” date on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
