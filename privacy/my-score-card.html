Applies to: My Score Card app

This Privacy Policy describes how the My Score Card app (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed to work primarily with data stored on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data on external servers.

Data you enter in the App (such as rounds, scores, putts, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

  • Data is stored locally on your device.
  • No account is required.
  • No data is uploaded or synced to Skyface servers.

3. Backup and Restore

The App may provide an optional manual backup/restore feature using local files (e.g., JSON). Backup files are created and managed by you. Skyface does not receive or store these files.

4. Sharing, Analytics, and Tracking

  • No selling or sharing of user data.
  • No third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.

5. Internet Access

Internet access is not required for the App’s core functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact:

  • Website: https://skyface.com
